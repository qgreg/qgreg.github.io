Next Best Everything

Next Best Everything offers users the power to curate ranked lists of things they are passionate about and anything else you can imagine!

The website uses Facebook login for security. Server-side PostgreSQL manages list storage and gameplay. Next Best Everything uses Celery to create realtime images to facilitate sharing of favorite items and list ranks.

To engage users and to support the site, background tasks find popular items on Amazon that match the user's categories and items.

Key technologies: python, flask, oauth, postgreSQL, SQLalchemy, Celery, Linode
