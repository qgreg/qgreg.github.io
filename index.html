Core Engineering Projects

C.C. Busy

Problem: Child care providers needed a faster way to log attendance, meals, diapering, and reminders without adding administrative overhead. Technology: Python, Flask, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy, Celery, OAuth, and Alexa integrations. Impact: Reduced manual recordkeeping and improved the flow of real-time information to providers and parents.

Untitled Pittsburgh Trash Pickup Voice App

Problem: Residents had to search through city schedule data to find pickup dates for trash, recycling, and yard debris. Technology: Python, Flask, Node.js, AWS Lambda, and Google Cloud Functions. Impact: Turned public data into a useful voice-driven civic utility and demonstrated practical serverless integration work.

Next Best Everything

Problem: Users needed a ranked-list platform with authentication, persistent data, and background processing for sharing and enrichment features. Technology: Python, Flask, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy, Celery, and OAuth. Impact: Showed end-to-end backend ownership across data modeling, asynchronous jobs, and product-facing feature delivery.
