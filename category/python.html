Restarting Supervisor Locally

You are developing your program, running it through a daemon via supervisor, serving it locally and testing it on your web browser. When you look at the status of your program, you find it is FATAL. Zip over to your editor, fix the bug, and you come back to restart your program and you get this error.

socket.error: [Errno 98] Address already in use

You've got to stop and start supervisor, but before you're done, you've got to hand kill a process. Stop supervisor:

sudo service supervisor stop

Get the process id of the remaining program:

ps -fA | grep python

Kill the process:

sudo kill <id>

Restart supervisor:

sudo service supervisor start
